The Journey

Arranging made simple.

We have replaced the stress of calling multiple directors with a calm, digital process. Here is how you can arrange a dignified farewell in under 20 minutes.

1

Search & Compare

Enter your postcode to see vetted directors near you. Filter by price, service type (Cremation/Burial), and "Merit Score." We show you the total price upfront—no hidden fees.

You See:

✓ Total Cost ✓ Facility Photos ✓ Audit Score
2

Build the Arrangement

Use our "Arrangement Builder" to select exactly what you want. Choose the coffin, transport, and music. This creates a digital plan that you can save, share with family, or edit later.

3

Connect with Confidence

Once you are ready, click "Connect." Your chosen director receives your plan and will call you to confirm the final logistics (like the date and time). No payment is taken online.
