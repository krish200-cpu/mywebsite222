Immediate Guidance

Breathe.
Take it one step at a time.

The first few days can feel overwhelming. We have broken down the legal and logistical requirements into a simple list. You do not need to do everything today.

Your Progress 0%

Phase 1: The First 24 Hours

Verify Life Extinct (VLE)

If at home, call the GP or 111. If at a hospital/care home, the staff will handle this automatically.

Notify Close Family

Inform immediate next of kin. Do not worry about wider announcements yet.

Locate the Will

Check for any specific funeral wishes or pre-paid plans before making arrangements.

Phase 2: Legal Formalities

Collect Medical Certificate

Obtain the Medical Certificate of Cause of Death (MCCD) from the doctor or bereavement office.

Book Registrar Appointment

You must register the death within 5 days. Book an appointment at the local Register Office.
