Est. 1984 • Independent Family Director

Our History

Founded by the Sterling family, Maritime Farewells began with a simple mission: to provide unique and dignified ceremonies for those who shared a lifelong bond with the sea. For over three decades, we have remained independent, focusing on relational care rather than corporate targets.

"We don't just arrange services; we honor journeys. Every life has a different rhythm, and we are here to help you find it."

Today, we are recognized as the UK's leading provider of burials at sea and eco-friendly maritime commemorations.
