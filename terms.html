Standard Terms of Service // Jan 2026

1.0 The Service Model

FuneralMatch acts as an independent auditing and matching intermediary. We provide families with vetted data on 1,200+ funeral directors nationwide. Users acknowledge that we are an information platform and not a direct provider of funeral services.

2.0 Director Obligations

Funeral Directors entering the network agree to maintain the "FuneralMatch Standard." This includes absolute pricing transparency and consenting to physical audits of mortuary facilities. Failure to maintain these standards results in immediate removal from the search results.

3.0 Lead Acquisition & Billing

Leads generated through the platform are exclusive. When a director claims a lead via the Director Portal, the transaction is debited from their Lead Credit balance. All sales of Lead Intelligence are final once the disclosure of PII (Personally Identifiable Information) occurs.

4.0 Limitation of Liability

While we vet every director to the highest degree, FuneralMatch is not liable for the final conduct of a director during a service. We provide the data; the family makes the choice based on that merit.
