The Care of Your Information

In your time of need, transparency is our primary mandate. When you submit an inquiry through our Emergency Help or Search tools, we collect specific data points—including names, contact details, and deceased information—to facilitate an immediate connection with a vetted director.

Lead Capture & Transmission

Your data is transmitted securely via our relay partner, Formspree, and stored within our encrypted Director Command Centre. We only disclose "Intelligence Data" to funeral directors who have:

  • Passed our physical facility audit.
  • Signed our ethical transparency agreement.
  • Paid the lead-acquisition fee to support the family search infrastructure.

Retention & Rights

We do not sell your personal data to third-party marketing firms. Information is used strictly for the purpose of matching you with a vetted funeral director. You have the right to request the immediate deletion of your data by contacting panchalkrish47@gmail.com.
